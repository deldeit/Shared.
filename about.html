How it works

A short overview of the main flow.

Step 1
Sign in and open the menu to access your profile, settings, or about page.
Step 2
Create a post by choosing an image, writing a caption, and selecting a track preview.
Step 3
Browse the feed, open profiles, and tap the like counter to view the list of likers.
Step 4
Use the menu to navigate quickly without losing the main feed context.

Privacy policy

This page explains, in plain language, how Shared handles information inside the app. It is a practical summary and not a substitute for a formal legal policy.

Information collected

  • Account details used to sign in.
  • Profile information such as display name and avatar.
  • Content you publish, including posts, captions, and likes.
  • Basic technical data needed to run the service.

How it is used

  • To create and show your profile and posts.
  • To display likes, authors, and verification status.
  • To help the app function, maintain sessions, and load content.
  • To improve usability and keep the app working correctly.

Sharing and visibility

  • Posts and profile details may be visible to other users inside the app.
  • Like lists can show who interacted with a post.
  • Content you publish should be treated as public within the app environment.

Retention and control

  • You may be able to update or remove some information through the app.
  • Deleted content may still remain in backups or logs for a limited time.
  • Account data is kept only as long as needed to operate the service and meet legal obligations.
