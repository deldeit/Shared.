star Shared

A simple place to post, share, and discover.

Shared is built around fast posting, profile discovery, and a feed that stays focused on the content. The experience is intentionally minimal: upload an image, add a caption, attach a song preview, and publish. Profiles stay visible, likes are interactive, and the menu keeps the most important actions one tap away.

How it works

A simple flow from login to posting.

Step 1
Sign in, then open your profile from the sidebar menu.
Step 2
Use the upload box to add an image, a caption, and a song preview.
Step 3
Publish the post and let others like, open the liker list, or visit your profile.
Step 4
Open About or Settings from the menu to move around the app quickly.

Notes

Shared is a compact social interface built to keep the important actions visible and fast. If you want to extend it later, the best places are the profile page, settings page, and the post detail flow.

Tip: keep the same black-and-white palette and rounded cards for a consistent experience across all pages.
